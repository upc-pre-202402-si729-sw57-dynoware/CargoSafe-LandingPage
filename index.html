About Us

About the Team

DynoWare is a startup led by a group of students from the Faculty of Engineering of the Universidad Peruana de Ciencias Aplicadas (UPC), focused on developing software and technological solutions aimed at transporters and entrepreneurs seeking the safe and undamaged arrival of their products.

About the Product

Through our CargoSafe platform we seek to provide tools for tracking, route management and optimization, and product management.

Efficiently manage cargo records.
